About Me

Hello! My name is Danielle, and I am currently studying Computer Science at Columbia University. In the past, I've created websites for Columbia University Formula Racing and Columbia University Spectator Publishing Company. I also have experience with app development and gaming engines. At Columbia, I conduct research on AR and 3D game development. I also worked on visualizations for storm surge flooding and for COVID-19 through performing data analysis and creating dashboards. I am currently working on another app with my 2nd research group to accompany self-administered blood-testing. Through this research, I have been introduced to machine learning. At Columbia, I am a product engineer for Spectator Publishing Company and served on the E-Board of Columbia University Formula Racing. My hobbies include running and peer mentorship.
